cartoons are a way of delivering a story: a progressive narrative or a thread of subjects, the main point is unfolding an idea.

collaborating with music artists has been a delightful chance to blend cartoon plots with a different medium of sound.

  • mooden the cat

    large-scale animation started with mooden the cat. a cartoon happened to be an appropriate way of telling

    a story about the character. in the video his name is explained, his habits & leisure described.

  • waiters — music video

    single from mad meg’s alum ‘who deserves balloons & medals’. the initial idea behind the video was bits &

    pieces of references, many of those based on gestures to mad meg discography & the lyrics.
